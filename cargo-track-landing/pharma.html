Frequently asked questions

Is Polarsat GDP-compliant?

Yes. The Validate and Certify tiers generate GDP, GMP, and WHO-compliant temperature documentation accepted by regulatory authorities.

How accurate are the temperature sensors?

Our EYE sensors measure temperature to ±0.3°C accuracy with readings every 10 seconds to 5 minutes depending on your plan.

What is included in the free hardware?

Every plan includes a Teltonika TAT140 GPS tracker, EYE environmental sensor, and a global SIM card with data for 5 years. Hardware is free with a 24-month commitment.

Can we integrate with our QMS or ERP?

Yes. The Certify tier includes full API access for integration with Quality Management Systems, ERP platforms, and warehouse systems.

What happens during a temperature excursion?

You receive instant alerts via SMS, email, and push notification. The Validate and Certify tiers include configurable escalation workflows with multiple notification levels.
