Frequently asked questions

Can we track assets in remote locations?

Yes. Our devices use LTE with global SIM coverage. They work in remote areas where most consumer devices lose signal.

Do you support hazardous goods compliance?

Yes. The Monitor and Command tiers include automated hazmat transport documentation and regulatory compliance reports.

What is included in the free hardware?

Every plan includes a Teltonika TAT140 GPS tracker, EYE environmental sensor, and a global SIM card with data for 5 years. Hardware is free with a 24-month commitment.

Can we integrate with SCADA systems?

Yes. The Command tier includes full API access for SCADA, ERP, and custom system integrations.

Can we upgrade our plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Locate to Monitor or Command at any time.
