Frequently asked questions

Are the trackers rugged enough for construction sites?

Yes. The Teltonika TAT140 is IP67-rated and designed for harsh environments including dust, water, and extreme temperatures.

Can we track equipment across multiple job sites?

Yes. All plans support unlimited job sites. The Optimise and Command tiers include multi-site management with site-level reporting.

What is included in the free hardware?

Every plan includes a Teltonika TAT140 GPS tracker, EYE environmental sensor, and a global SIM card with data for 5 years. Hardware is free with a 24-month commitment.

How does theft prevention work?

Geofencing, after-hours movement detection, and tamper alerts work together to detect unauthorised equipment movement instantly. You receive alerts via SMS, email, and push notification.

Can we integrate with our construction ERP?

Yes. The Command tier includes full API access for integration with Procore, Buildertrend, and other construction management platforms.
