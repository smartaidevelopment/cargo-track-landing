Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Data Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Account information (email, company name, phone)
  • Device tracking data (location, sensor readings)
  • Payment information (processed securely through payment gateways)
  • Usage data and analytics

2. Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Provide tracking and monitoring services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send alerts and notifications
  • Improve our services

3. Data Protection

We implement:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security audits

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data export
  • Request data deletion
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Object to data processing

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Inactive accounts may be deleted after 90 days of inactivity.

6. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: privacy@cargotrackpro.com
