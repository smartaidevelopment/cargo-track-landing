Frequently asked questions

How quickly can we start tracking?

Most teams complete setup the same day. Devices arrive pre-configured — just power on and attach.

Can we track both vehicles and shipments?

Yes. You can track vehicle-level movement and shipment-level events with sensor alerts and status timelines.

What is included in the free hardware?

Every plan includes a Teltonika TAT140 GPS tracker, EYE environmental sensor, and a global SIM card with data for 5 years. Hardware is free with a 24-month commitment.

Can we upgrade our plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Track to Monitor or Predict at any time. Downgrading is available at your next billing cycle.

Do you support API integrations?

Full API access is included in the Predict tier. Monitor tier includes webhook-based integrations.
