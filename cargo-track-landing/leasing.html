Frequently asked questions

Can lessees see the tracking data?

You control data access. Lessees can be given limited visibility or no access at all, depending on your preference.

How does repo support work?

The Protect tier provides real-time GPS location and last-known position for any asset. You can share location links with recovery agents directly from the dashboard.

What is included in the free hardware?

Every plan includes a Teltonika TAT140 GPS tracker, EYE environmental sensor, and a global SIM card with data for 5 years. Hardware is free with a 24-month commitment.

Can we integrate with our lease management system?

Yes. The Protect tier includes full API access for integration with lease management platforms, accounting systems, and portfolio tools.

What happens if a device is tampered with?

The Manage and Protect tiers include tamper detection alerts. You receive instant notifications if a device is disconnected or moved unexpectedly.
