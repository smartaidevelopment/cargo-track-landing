Frequently asked questions

How does the risk scoring work?

Our AI risk engine analyses six factors — delays, temperature excursions, speed anomalies, battery health, geofence violations, and signal loss — to produce a weighted risk score from 0-100 for each device and fleet-wide.

Can we integrate with our underwriting systems?

Yes. The Predict tier includes full API access for integration with underwriting platforms, policy management systems, and claims workflows.

What is included in the free hardware?

Every plan includes a Teltonika TAT140 GPS tracker, EYE environmental sensor, and a global SIM card with data for 5 years. Hardware is free with a 24-month commitment.

How does usage-based pricing work?

Fleet risk scores are mapped to premium multipliers: Low risk (0.7x), Medium (1.0x), High (1.3x), Critical (1.6x). Lower-risk fleets pay lower premiums based on verified operational data.

Can we offer this to our policyholders?

Yes. We offer white-label and partner programmes for insurers who want to deploy tracking devices to their policyholders' fleets.
